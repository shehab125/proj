About Alpha Box

Our Vision

Alpha Box (Smart Medical Box) is an innovative graduation project developed by a team of 10 students in the Artificial Intelligence Department. The project aims to develop a smart system that measures and monitors patient vital signs using sensors connected to a medical box, then analyzes this data using artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques.

Project Components

The project consists of a hardware component (a medical box containing sensors) and a software component (backend server, database, AI models, and user interface). The project aims to facilitate remote patient monitoring, reduce the time needed to diagnose critical cases, and support medical decision-making using artificial intelligence.

Hardware

Advanced medical sensors and IoT devices for accurate measurements

AI Models

Machine learning algorithms for data analysis and prediction

Cloud Platform

Secure cloud storage and real-time data processing

Key Features

  • Continuous monitoring of vital signs
  • AI-powered health predictions
  • Instant alerts for critical conditions
  • Cloud-based data storage and analysis
  • User-friendly interface for medical staff

Our Team

Alpha Box is developed by a dedicated team of 10 students from the Artificial Intelligence Department at Helwan International Technology University. Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, software development, hardware engineering, and medical technology to create an innovative solution for healthcare monitoring.
